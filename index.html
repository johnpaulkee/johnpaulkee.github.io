Hi, I'm John Paul Kee.


I am an undergraduate student in the University of British Columbia specializing in Computer Science.

I started designing websites in 2014 when I started volunteering for a club called UBC Blood For Life. I created my first website for this club to help broadcast the importance of Stem Cell Research. Since then, I've been self teaching myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

In my university courses, I have been taught programming languages such as C++, Java, and DrRacket (a variant of LISP).

Today, I am working as a Teaching Assistant in the UBC Computer Science Faculty.

During my free time, I'm often learning how to develop mobile applications on the iPhone. I'm currently studying the Swift Programming Language that Apple has just released.
